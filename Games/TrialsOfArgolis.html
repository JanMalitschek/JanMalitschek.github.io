Trials of Argolis

Trials of Argolis is a First-Person meele combat Bossrush set in Ancient Greece mixed with some fantasy elements.
Inspired by popular combat games like Dark Souls and Sekiro it combines First-Person movement
with fast paced melee combat requiring quick reflexes and well timed reactions.
The game can be found here

Trials of Argolis is by far my most ambitious project to date. It started as a procedural open world boss rush in a PSX style went through multiple prototyping stages including one complete rewrite from scratch until I finally settled on the game it has become now.
The final game was created in about 6 months from start to finish including all the required assets such as textures(which were handpainted), models, animation, SFX and OST.
It has definitely taught me the most in terms of character animations since almost every single boss(there are 15 of them) has a unique moveset.

It is important to mention that this was a complete solo project apart from the help I with QA Testing and fixing a performance issue.
