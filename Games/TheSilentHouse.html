The Silent House

A game about a drug investigator who get's dragged into something much worse than he anticipated.
This game started as a fun little project during the winter holidays of 2016. At some point I decided
to turn it into a finished game and released a demo version which got pretty popular.
The game can be found here

In the process of making this game I was able to explore many new techniques in level design and programming.
In retrospective it does have many flaws but it still taught me a lot of valuable lessons in many fields of
gamedesign and lead me to meeting a bunch of awesome people who I still work with today.

It is important to mention that this was a complete solo project up until the release of the Anniversary Update, where I got help with ideas and writing the story.
